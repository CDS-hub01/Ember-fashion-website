The Beginning

A House Built on Contrast

EMBER was founded in Port Harcourt in 2019 with one belief — that Nigerian luxury deserved its own voice. Not an imitation of European fashion houses. Not a copy of what already existed. Something entirely its own.

Every collection begins with a single question: what does it feel like to be powerful and quiet at the same time? The answer lives in every piece we make.
